About

My name is Genevieve Stevens, and I am a passionate and motivated Junior Front-End Web Developer with a keen eye for design and a strong desire to create engaging and intuitive user experiences. Before I pursued web development, I was a musical theatre performer, contemporary dancer, and choreographer with an honors BFA from Toronto Metropolitan University. My journey in web development began during the pandemic when I attended Sheridan’s Front End Web Developer program. Throughout the Sheridan program I fell in love with combining creativity and technology to bring ideas to life. After my developer program, I undertook several projects to hone my skills in creating responsive, visually appealing websites. I have experience working with modern frameworks and libraries such as React and Bootstrap, enabling me to build dynamic and interactive web applications. Beyond coding, I have a strong passion for design and user interface (UI)/user experience (UX) principles. I believe that a seamless and aesthetically pleasing design is essential for user engagement and satisfaction. I thrive in a collaborative environment where creativity and teamwork are encouraged. I am reliable and conscientious with excellent communication and organization skills (providing realistic timelines and innovative options) the last bit is optional. I'm constantly expanding my skill set, staying up-to-date with the latest trends and best practices in the ever-evolving world of front-end development.
